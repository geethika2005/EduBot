Admissions Process

Welcome to the admissions page of our engineering college. Here, you will find all the necessary information regarding the admissions process, eligibility criteria, important dates, and required documents.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 60% marks in 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
  • Valid score in the engineering entrance examination

Important Dates

  • Application Start Date: 1st July
  • Application End Date: 31st July
  • Entrance Exam Date: 15th August
  • Results Announcement: 31st August

Required Documents

  • 10th and 12th Mark Sheets
  • Entrance Exam Scorecard
  • Passport Size Photographs
  • Photo ID Proof

How to Apply

Step Description
Step 1 Fill out the online application form on our official website.
Step 2 Upload the required documents.
Step 3 Pay the application fee online.
Step 4 Submit the application form.
